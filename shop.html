The Owl's Nest

The Owl's Nest in Aviemore is a boutique hotel in a perfect location good for skiing, restaurants and shopping. Guests can choose from a range of beautiful rooms with private en-suites, excellent amenities and scenes of the stunning Pitlochry.

Hazeldean Cabin

£180 per night

Hazeldean Cabin is a fantastic choice for pet-lovers! This cosy cabin near Inverness features a hot tub, Netflix and fire pit. The cabin features a king-size bed, well stocked kitchenette, comfy dog bed and dog treats for your four legged friend.

Hillfoot

£326 per night

Hillfoot is a stunning self-catering lodge sitting just outside of Pitlochry. It features a BBQ, fully functioning kitchen and a hot tub. Fantastic walks are available nearby and guests will be greeted with a welcome basket stuffed with Scottish treats including shortbread and small batch Scottish gin.
